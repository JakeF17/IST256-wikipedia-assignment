Jacob Fought (born February 8th 2005) is the son of Paula and Bryan Fought. Jacob graduated from Millville High School, Millville PA, in 2023. In High School he played soccer, baseball, and basketball. He received all conference honors in soccer and baseball. After graduation, he continued playing soccer at Penn State Berks. In addition to soccer, Jacob studies Cybersecurity analytics and operations. He began becoming interested in computers over covid when he built his PC. Since then, he has enjoyed playing video games such as Valorant, Rocket League, and Rainbow 6 Siege. Jacob's other passions include golfing, camping, and working out. After college, he hopes to work as a software engineer.

Childhood

Jacob is the youngest of four children. He has two older sisters named Rebecca and Jaycie, as well as an older brother named John. Growing up, he spent most of his time playing outdoors. He played games like wiffle ball, kickball, and wall ball. He has had multiple dogs growing up, the main 3 being Beasley, Cali, and Kun.
